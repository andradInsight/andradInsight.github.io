Learning


The AP Program is recognized internationally, with over twenty-two thousand schools registered, and over one million graduates in total. The program encourages its participants to develop their skills in an enviornment more challenging than usual classes.

Students who study in AP classes study to prepare for university-level courses, starting right from the beginning of their junior year. The exams that are given by the end of their 12th grade are accepted by universities and colleges worldwide.
