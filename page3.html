Bienvenue!

Welcome to the Father Jorg Department of Math! This page is reserved for students either curious about math-related stuffs, staff in charge of maintaining and updating the site, or staff in the department itself.

This department is concerned with the well-being of all math-related staffs as well as their students. Father Jorg takes pride in its academics, and math is no exception. With revered Mr. Sub as the lead administrator and teacher in charge, the math department pulls no strings in its pursuit of knowledge - hence our department nickname, "The Ivory Tower".

The Math Department is notorious for its vigourous exams(see Advanced Placement), its club governed by said teachers, and the Math Competition, which is held annually with other schools in Toronto.

Learning

Father Jorgs' is always open to the learning process of its students. From group excursions to competitions, our students is always striving to learn more about the world around us. With the Faculty of Math, students here at Father Jorg will always reach their goals.

Our Math Department offers many programs, all which strengthen students' capabilities, like problem-solving, communication, and learning skills, which are invaluable in day-to-day life.

The Math Competition come every year, with runner ups Dao and Yu being our top achievers!
